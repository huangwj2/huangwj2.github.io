Welcome to Dr. Huang's Practice of Chinese Medicine and Acupuncture

Dr. rer. med. Wenjing Huang, who has been trained both in China and Germany, offers you integrative treatments based on evidence-based traditional Chinese medicine and acupuncture.

Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM)is a systematic discipline that has contributed the health of people in China over thousands of years. It treats not only the diseases but also plays a key - role in prevention and improving healthcare. TCM emphasizes on prevention, physical and mental health, as well as individualized treatment. With the positive results of scientific research on Chinese medicine and acupuncture in recent years, TCM has gained more trust and acceptance in the Western world.


Dr. Wenjing Huangstudied the traditional Chinese medicine for seven years at the Chengdu University of TCM and completed a doctorate with a focus on acupuncture research at Charité - Universitätsmedizin Berlin. She has over 20 years of professional experience in Chinese medicine and acupuncture. Since 2019, she has been practicing as a naturopath and in July 2020 she took over the Acupuncture Centre Kurfürstendamm Berlin.
